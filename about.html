About

Culture according to the language board dictionary "Kamus Dewan" means the whole way of life as well as all the results of activities and creations in the form of material or spirituality of a society. Culture affects human attitudes and behavior in other words all humans are cultural actors because humans behave within the scope of culture.
Language according to the language board dictionary "Kamus Dewan" means a system of sound symbols used as a communication tool within a group of people.

Club logo

The establishment of this club is to develop the student's potential in language mastery. In the meantime, it also allows students to recognize and understand the cultures that are found in Malaysia, further fostering feelings of love and appreciation for cultural differences.
